================================================================================
LEARNING SYSTEM EXAMPLE
================================================================================

Scenario: 100 signals generated over 1 week

1. Each signal recorded with: entry, stops, confluences list
2. Every 5 min: check current price vs TP1/TP2/SL
3. When signal closes: update CSV with outcome (TP1_HIT, TP2_HIT, LOSS, EXPIRED)

Analysis after 1 week (50 closed signals):
  RSI Divergence: present in 40/50 winners → 80% win rate
  Order Flow Imbalance: present in 42/50 winners → 84% win rate
  MACD Crossover: present in 18/50 winners → 36% win rate

Weight Adjustment:
  RSI Divergence: 2.0 → 2.0 × 1.05 = 2.1 (boost, >60%)
  Order Flow Imbalance: 2.0 → 2.0 × 1.05 = 2.1 (boost, >60%)
  MACD Crossover: 1.0 → 1.0 × 0.90 = 0.9 (penalize, <40%)

Result: Next week's signals weight RSI Divergence + Order Flow more heavily,
       reduce MACD impact. System adapts to what's working.
